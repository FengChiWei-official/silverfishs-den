## preparation
- let $G$ and $G^{\prime}$ are two groups
- Let $\phi: G \to G^{\prime}$ a map
## define 
[[Homomorphism]] is the relationship of
$$
\begin{align}
&\forall a, b \in G, \\
&\quad \phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)
\end{align}
$$
and the $\phi$ map is the [[Homomorphism]]
