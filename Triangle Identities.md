## Definition

$\sin^2 x + \cos^2 x = 1$

$\sec^2 x - \tan^2 x = 1$ 
> $\frac {\cos^2 x}{\cos^2 x} = \frac{1 - \sin^2 x}{\cos^2 x}$

$\csc^2 x - \cot^2 x = 1$ 
> $\frac {\sin^2 x}{\sin^2 x} = \frac{1 - \cos^2 x}{\sin^2 x}$
