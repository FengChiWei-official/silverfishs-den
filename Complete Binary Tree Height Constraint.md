## Define
A [[Complete Binary Tree|CBT]] with `n` nodes, its height `H` is always:
$$
\mathbf{H} = \lfloor\log_2n\rfloor+1
$$
## How do I remember it?
A [[Tree]]'s height is always `log` based, and binary means `2`, so it is trivial to believe that $\mathbf{H}=\lfloor\log_2n\rfloor+i$.
And let n be `2`, you will firmly believe that the constant `i` is `1`.
