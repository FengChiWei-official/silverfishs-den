## Text

- **[[Project]] (动词):** To extend outward or forward as a structural, intentional, or natural continuation of a main body.  
    _(作为一个主体结构在空间上具有功能性、设计感或自然规律的**向外延伸**。)_
- **[[Protrude]] (动词):** To push or poke through a surface, boundary, or opening in an awkward, abnormal, or intrusive way.  
    _(打破原本的表面或边界，突兀、异常且往往不合时宜地**戳出来**。)_
- **[[Stand Out]] (短语动词):** To be highly visible, distinct, or prominent compared to the surrounding environment.  
    _(不论空间上有没有位移，在视觉上或性质上与周围形成巨大反差而**脱颖而出**。)_
