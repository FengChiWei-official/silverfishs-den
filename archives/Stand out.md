## Text

> phrase v.

1. If something stands out from a surface, it [rises](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/rise "rises 的释义") up from it.
	1. Her hair stood out in spikes.
2. If something [stands](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/stand "stands 的释义") out, it is very [noticeable](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/noticeable "noticeable 的释义").
	1. Every tree, wall and fence stood out against dazzling white fields.
3. If something stands out, it is much [better](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/better "better 的释义") or much more [important](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/important "important 的释义") than other things of the same [kind](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/zh/dictionary/english/kind "kind 的释义").
	1. He played the violin, and he stood out from all the other musicians.
