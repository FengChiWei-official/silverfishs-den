## Definition

$$e = \lim_{x \to \infty} (1+\frac{1}{x})^x$$

>[!note] 
> $\lim_{x \to \infty} (1+\frac{1}{x})^{x^{f(x)}} \ne (\lim_{x \to \infty}(1+\frac{1}{x})^{x})^{f(x)} \ne e^{f(x)}$
