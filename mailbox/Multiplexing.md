## qwq
**Multiplexing (MUX)** is a method by which multiple analog or digital signals are combined into one signal over a shared medium. The goal is to maximize the utilization of expensive communication infrastructure. The reverse process at the receiver is called **Demultiplexing (DEMUX)**.
