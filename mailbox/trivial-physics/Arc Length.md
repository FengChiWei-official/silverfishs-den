## Definition

### Geometric

Arc Length is defined as the **limit of the total length of a rectifying polygonal line**.

$\mid \Delta s_i \mid = \sqrt{\Delta x_i^2 + \Delta y_i^2}$

$S = \sum_{\Delta s_i \text{ in the interval}} \mid \Delta s_i \mid$



### Physic

Arc Length can also be defined as the **accumulated distance traveled by a moving particle over a time interval**.
