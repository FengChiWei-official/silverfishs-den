## Definition
It gives a **Upper Boundary** for Productions.
### Type 1
$$
\sqrt[n]{\prod a_i} \le \frac{\sum a_i}{n}
$$
**Factorial Logic** $\sqrt[n]{\prod_1^n}$ = $\sqrt[n]{n!}$. It is Bounded by **Arithmetic Mean** $\frac{k (n + 1)}{2 \dot k} = \frac{n+1} {2}$.
