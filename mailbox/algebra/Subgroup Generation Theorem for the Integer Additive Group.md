## Definition
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\mathbf{S} = \mathbb{Z}a + \mathbb{Z}b, \\
&&\text{where } \mathbb{Z}a + \mathbb{Z}b = \{ma + nb \mid m , n\in \mathbb{Z} \}
\end{aligned}
$$
$\mathbf{S}$ is a [[Subgroup]] of [[Additive Group of Integers]]($\mathbb{Z}, +$).
