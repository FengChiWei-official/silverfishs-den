## Definition
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathbf{S} &= \mathbb{Z}a + \mathbb{Z}b, \\
d &= \gcd(a, b), \\
(\mathbf{S}, +) &\cong (\mathbb{Z}d, +).
\end{aligned}
$$
