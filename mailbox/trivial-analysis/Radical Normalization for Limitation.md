## Definition

If $\sqrt{a} - \sqrt{b} = 0$ and $\sqrt{a} + \sqrt{b} \ne 0$, we got


$$
\begin{align}
&&& \sqrt{a} - \sqrt{b} \\
&= && \frac{(\sqrt{a})^2 - (\sqrt{b})^2}{\sqrt{a} + \sqrt{b}} \\
& = && \frac{a-b}{\sqrt{a}  + \sqrt{b}} \\

\end {align}
$$
