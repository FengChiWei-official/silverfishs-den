## Definition

German articles indicate definiteness, gender, number, and case. Core sets include definite articles (der/die/das), indefinite articles (ein/eine), and negation/article-like forms (kein).

## Quick Table

- Definite: der (M), die (F), das (N), die (Pl)
- Indefinite: ein (M/N), eine (F)
- Negation: kein/keine

## Notes

- Articles change form for case and number; they are central to noun phrase agreement and are taught alongside declension patterns.
