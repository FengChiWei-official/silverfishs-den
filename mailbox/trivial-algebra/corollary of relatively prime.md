## pre-define
$a,b$ is two integer

## define
$$
\begin{align}
&\text{a,b is relatively prime} \\
&\quad\text{ if and only if: }\\
&\quad\exists r,s \in \mathbf{Z}, \text{ s.t. } ra+sb=1
\end{align}
$$
## proof

$ra+sb \in \mathbf{Z}a+\mathbf{Z}b = \mathbf{Z}d$, which is a subgroup of $(\mathbf{Z}, +)$. so min positive of it $d$ is $\text{gcd}(a,b)$. 
so
if a, b relatively prime, $\text{gcd}(a,b)=1$ ,so $1 \in \mathbf{Z}d$. 
and 
1 is min of $\mathbf{Z}^+$, so 1 is the gcd, so a, b is relatively prime.
