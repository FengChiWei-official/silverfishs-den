## Definition

When the data's range is fixed, you can separate them in to Buckets.
> [!note]
> i.e.
> range: [0.0, 10000.0]
> buckets: [0.0, 1000.0], ..., [9000.0, 10000.0]

If you're going to search for **9001.23**, you search range is [9000, 10000].
