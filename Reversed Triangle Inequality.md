## Definition

Following 2 inequality hold simultaneously.
1. $\mid a \mid - \mid b \mid \: \le \: \mid a - b \mid$
2. $\mid b \mid - \mid a \mid \: \le \: \mid a - b \mid$
Combining these two inequalities, we obtain:
$$\mid \, \mid a \mid - \mid b \mid\, \mid \: \le \: \mid a - b \mid$$
Which is equivalent to:
$$-|\alpha - \beta| \le |\alpha| - |\beta| \le |\alpha - \beta|$$
