## qwq
$$\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
x & \equiv a_1 \pmod{m_1} \\
x & \equiv a_2 \pmod{m_2} \\
  & \vdots                  \\
x & \equiv a_i \pmod{m_i}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}$$
has solution if and only if 
for all i, j
$$ a_i \equiv q_j \pmod{\text{gcd}(m_i, m_j)}$$
