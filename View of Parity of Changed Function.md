## Perspectives

Given $f(x)$,
1. Decomposition
	1. Even one $f(x)+f(-x)$
	2. Odd one $f(x)-f(-x)$
	3. $f(x) = \frac{f(x) + f(-x)}{2} + \frac{f(x) - f(-x)}{2}$
2. Even $f(\mid x \mid)$
	1. generalize
	2. $\text{Any}(\text{Even}(x))$  is Even
3. Same with $f(x)$:  $f(x^3)$
	1. generalize
	2. $\text{any}(\text{Odd}(x))$ is the same with $f(x)$
	3. $x$ is a Odd function.
4. Same with $f(x)$: $f^{-1}(x)$
5. Exchange: $\frac{d}{dx} f(x)$
6. Exchange: $\int (\cdot) dx (f(x))$
7. Must be Odd: $f(x+y) = f(x) + f(y)$: [[Cauchy Functional Equation]]
