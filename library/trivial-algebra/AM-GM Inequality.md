## Definition
$$
\begin{align}

&\text{For }a_i > 0, \\
&\frac{\sum a_i}{n} \ge \sqrt[n]{\prod a_i} \\
& \text{equality holds iff } a_1 = a_2 = \dots = a_n\\



\end {align}
$$
