## Definition
**Isomorphism** is a [[Bijective Map]], a Bijective [[Homomorphism]]

## Hyponym
[[Automorphism]].

## How to recognize?
Based on [[Corollary on ralationship between injective and kernel]], we know that if the [[Kernel of Homomorphism|kernel]] is trivial($\{1_{\mathbf{G}}\}$), the [[Homomorphism]] is a [[Bijective Map]].
