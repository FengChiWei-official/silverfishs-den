## define
A [[Subgroup]] $\mathbf{N}$ of Group $mathbf{G}$ is a [[Normal Subgroup]],  if
$\forall a \in N$, $\forall g \in \mathbf{G}$, then the [[Conjugate]] $gag^{-1} \in N$
