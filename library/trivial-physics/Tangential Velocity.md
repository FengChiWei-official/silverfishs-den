## Definition
[[Tangential Velocity]] is the component of [[Acceleration]] that is [[Parallel]] to the [[Instantaneous Velocity]].

Its notation is $\vec a _t$.
It is response to [[Unit Tangential Vector]]

$$
\begin {align}

&  \vec a_t && = \vec a \hat{T} \\
&   && = \frac {\vec a \vec v}{\mid \vec v \mid}\\
&   && = \frac{dv}{dt}\\

\end {align}
$$

> [!tip]
> $\vec a_t$ is easy to get from $\vec v$. So you normally calculate $\vec a_n$ by it and $\vec a$.
