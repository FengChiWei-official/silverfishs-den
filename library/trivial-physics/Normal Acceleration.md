## Definition
[[Normal Acceleration]] is the component of [[Acceleration]] that is [[Perpendicular]] to the [[Instantaneous Velocity]]. It is responsible for changing the direction of the [[Velocity]].

It is Notation is $\vec{a}_n$.

It can be defined by [[Principal Normal Vector]]($\hat N$).

## fff
$$
\begin{align}
&\vec{a}_n && = \vec a \hat N\\
&&&= \frac { \mid  \vec{v} \times \vec{a} \mid } {v} \\
&&& = \frac {v^2} {R} = \omega ^ 2 R \\
&&& = \sqrt{\vec{a}^2 - \vec{a}_t^2} \\
\end {align}
$$
