## Definition

$$
\begin {align}

&\hat N &&= \quad \mid \mid \frac {d\hat T}{ds} \mid\mid \\

&&& =  \frac{d \hat T / ds}{\mid \mid d \hat T / ds \mid \mid} \\

&&& = \frac{d \hat T / dt}{\mid \mid d \hat T / dt \mid \mid} \\
\end {align}

$$

And $\frac {d \hat T} {ds}$ is the value of [[Curvature]]($\kappa$).
