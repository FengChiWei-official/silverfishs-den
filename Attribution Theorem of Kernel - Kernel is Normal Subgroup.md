## define
[[Kernel of Homomorphism|Kernel]] is a [[Normal Subgroup]]

## proof
1. kernel is a group
2. $\forall a \in K, \phi(gag^{-1}) = \phi(g)\phi(a)\phi(g^{-1})=\phi(g)1\phi(g^{-1})=1$
3. thus, $\forall a \in K, gag^{-1} \in K$
4. Q.E.D
