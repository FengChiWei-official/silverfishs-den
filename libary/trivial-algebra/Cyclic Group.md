## In a word
A [[Group]] that can be generated by a single [[Generator of Cyclic Group|Generator]]

## Definition
$\mathbf{A^{\prime}}$ is a [[Cyclic Subgroup]],

$\mathbf{A}$ is a [[Cyclic Group]] if and only if 
$$
\exists \mathbf{A}^{\prime},\text{ s.t. }\mathbf{A} = \mathbf{A}^{\prime}
$$
