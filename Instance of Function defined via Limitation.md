## Text

$f(x) = \lim_{t \to +\infty} \frac{x+e^{tx}}{1+ e^{tx}}$ ,

**Step 1**: Consider `x` to be a parameter. the base on
	the property of [[Exponentiation]]'s parameter, partition domain of  `x` is $\{[-\infty, 0), \{0\}, (0, \infty] \}$

**Step 2**: Solve the  limitation, get the [[Piece-wise Function]].

$$
f(x) =
\left \{
\begin{align}
&x, &&x < 0 \\
&\frac{1}{2}, && x = 0 \\
& 1, && x > 0 \\
\end{align}
\right .
$$
