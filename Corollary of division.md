## preparation
- $p$ is a prime.
- $a, b$ are integers
## define
$p$ divides $ab$,then
$p$ divides $a$ or $p$ divides b.

## proof
suppose $p$ cannot divide $a$.
consider set$\mathbf{Z}k = \mathbf{Z}a + \mathbf{Z}p$, we know $ra+sp = 1$
so, $rab + spb = b$. therefore, $rmp+sbp = b \to (rm+sb)p = b$
so, p is a factor of b.
